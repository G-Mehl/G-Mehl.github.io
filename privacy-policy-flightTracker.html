Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android Flight Tracker app developed as part of a student project and published for demonstration purposes on the Google Play Store.

1. No Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It does not require or access any permissions (e.g., location, contacts, camera, etc.).

2. Flight Data Source

All flight data displayed in the app is retrieved in real-time from the OpenSky Network API, which provides open, non-commercial access to flight tracking information.

The app uses this data solely for educational and non-commercial purposes.

3. No Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out via the contact section of my portfolio website.

This policy may be updated if the app is modified in the future to collect or use data in any way.
